## Choosing Init
You can choose of two different inits: NeoInit and RInit
NOTE: NeoInit and RInit have a bit different shell.
### NeoInit
Pros:
* Simple
* Lightweight
* Very easy to manage services
* C-Written
Cons:
* May be not enough functions
### RInit
Pros:
* Fast
* Working with blocks
* C-Written

Cons:
* Bigger than NeoInit
* No service managing tools
